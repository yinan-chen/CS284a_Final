Week of

  • During the first half of week1, team members should utilize the resource to get familiar with the tools, math and algorithms that this project requires. For example, members should get a better understanding about Navier-Stokes equations and try to write simple programs with Three.js.
  • After getting familiar with the tools and maths, the team will meet up to divide the work and start coding separately.

Week of

  • The team will continue to work on the code, and integrate different parts after they are finished. If there are any problems that are hard to solve, members should help each other and do pair programming to solve the problems.

Week of

  • At the beginning of week3, most parts of the project should be integrated together. The team will continue to integrate, test the program and fix bugs.
  • Working on the milestone report and video.
  • If everything is working fine, the team will try to polish the program by making the algorithm more efficient or improving the interaction between user and the program.
